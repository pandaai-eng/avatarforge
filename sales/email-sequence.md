# AvatarForge Email Sequence

## Email 1 - The Problem
Subject: Most AI avatars fail for one reason

Most people are building AI avatars backwards.
They obsess over the image.
Then wonder why the content feels flat and the offer does not convert.

What actually matters is the system behind the avatar:
- identity
- visual consistency
- voice
- scripts
- CTA logic
- packaging

That’s why I built AvatarForge.

## Email 2 - The Mechanism
Subject: The real moat isn’t the render

The moat is having a reusable avatar operating system.
Not one prompt.
Not one pretty image.
A system.

AvatarForge helps you build exactly that with niche presets, prompt frameworks, content assets, and launch materials.

## Email 3 - The Use Case
Subject: Use it yourself or sell it

One thing I like about AvatarForge:
You can use it internally for your own content stack.
Or you can package the same system into a product or service.

That gives it leverage.
